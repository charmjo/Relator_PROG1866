Her Country

The Philippines is a tropical, archipelagic country located in Southeast Asia. It’s composed of 7,641 islands which are grouped into three: Luzon, Visayas and Mindanao. Not only that, it is known for its beautiful seascapes and landscapes.

While located in Southeast Asia, it shares a lot of its culture with Latin America owing to 300 years of Spanish Influence. One of which is its main religion, Roman Catholicism. These means PLENTY of festivals throughout the year celebrating the aspects of the Catholic faith. One of its most famous festivals includes Sinulog in Cebu. [1]

Aside from its Hispanic culture, it also shares many parts of its culture with its Southeast Asian brethren when it comes to religion (Islam) and food. Aside from them, Chinese culture is a huge influence to the Philippine’s culinary arts. Examples of food are pancit, and siopao.[2]
